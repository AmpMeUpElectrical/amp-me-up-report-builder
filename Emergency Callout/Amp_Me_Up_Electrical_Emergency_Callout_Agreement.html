4.Nature of Fault Finding

The client understands that fault finding is an investigative process and:

  • The duration of works cannot be guaranteed or accurately estimated prior to commencement.
  • Additional faults may be identified during testing.
  • Further repairs, parts, or return visits may be required.

5.Authority to Proceed

By signing this agreement, the client authorises Amp Me Up Electrical to:

  • Commence fault finding immediately upon arrival.
  • Carry out all necessary testing and diagnostic procedures.
  • Undertake minor repairs as required to make the installation safe.

Any major or non-essential works will be discussed prior to proceeding where reasonably practicable.

6.Compliance, Safety & Duty of Care

All works will be carried out in accordance with applicable Australian Standards and regulations, including AS/NZS 3000:2018, the Queensland Electrical Connection Manual (QECM) 2024, and any other relevant standards and statutory requirements.

AS/NZS 3000 requires non-compliant electrical installations to be rectified when the circuit they form part of is altered, added to, or replaced. This is particularly relevant in older residential properties where existing wiring, switchboards, earthing arrangements, or installations may not meet current standards. Where works commence on a circuit or installation subsequently found to contain non-compliance outside the original scope, additional rectification works may be legally required before the works can be safely completed; such works will be discussed and charged at the applicable callout rates, or, where mandated by immediate safety, carried out and notified at the earliest opportunity.

The client acknowledges and agrees that:

  • Amp Me Up Electrical has a legal duty of care to ensure all electrical work complies with the above standards.
  • Temporary or non-compliant solutions will not be installed for the purpose of restoring power where doing so would breach regulations or create a safety risk.
  • If a fault cannot be rectified within the expected timeframe, temporary measures cannot be made in order to restore power, and power may remain isolated until compliant repairs can be completed.
  • No work will be carried out that may compromise safety, or risk the electrical licence of the worker and/or company, for convenience.

7.No Guarantee of Immediate Resolution

All reasonable efforts will be made to identify and rectify the issue; however, resolution during the initial visit is not guaranteed.

8.Cease Work by Client

The client may request that work be ceased at any time. If work is ceased at the client's request:

  • The installation will be left in a safe condition.
  • All labour time and materials used up to that point will be invoiced in accordance with the rates and terms outlined in this agreement, regardless of whether the work has been completed.

9.Change of Mind / Cancellation

The client acknowledges and agrees that:

  • If Amp Me Up Electrical has been engaged and has commenced travel to site, a minimum fee of $ excluding GST will be charged.
  • If Amp Me Up Electrical arrives on site and the client cancels or declines to proceed, the full first-hour rate applicable to the time of attendance will be charged.

10.Site Access & Safety

The client agrees to provide safe, clear, and unobstructed access to all required areas, including switchboards, ceiling spaces, and subfloors. Where furniture, appliances, stored items, vehicles, or other obstructions need to be moved by Amp Me Up Electrical to carry out the works safely and efficiently, additional charges may apply. The client is responsible for ensuring that pets are restrained or kept in a safe location for the duration of the works, and that children are kept clear of work areas. Where the client arranges for property access in their absence, the client accepts responsibility for any issues arising from that arrangement.

11.Roof and Building Damage

Electrical work involving tin and tiled roofs carries an inherent risk of incidental damage, including cracked roof tiles, dented roof sheets, and cosmetic surface markings. While all reasonable care will be taken, Amp Me Up Electrical is not responsible for the replacement of these building materials. Where roof penetrations are required, they will be sealed using appropriate weatherproof methods; however, we cannot guarantee against future leaks caused by pre-existing roof condition, building movement, or factors outside our control.

12.Client-Supplied Equipment

Where client-supplied fittings or equipment are installed, Amp Me Up Electrical accepts no responsibility for manufacturer defects, incompatibility issues, premature product failure, or warranty claims relating to those items. Additional labour charges may apply where client-supplied products are found unsuitable or require modification for installation. Where a fault or failure occurs with a client-supplied item installed by Amp Me Up Electrical and it is not immediately clear whether the fault is due to the installation or the product itself, Amp Me Up Electrical will attend and investigate; if the fault is found to be a manufacturer or product defect rather than an installation issue, all associated costs — including call-out, travel time, and time on site — will be charged at the standard rate, and the client's recourse is against the product manufacturer or supplier. Amp Me Up Electrical reserves the right to refuse installation of any client-supplied item that, in our professional judgement, is unsafe, non-compliant, in poor condition, or unsuitable for the intended application; suitable replacements can be supplied at additional cost.

13.Workmanship Warranty

Amp Me Up Electrical warrants its workmanship for the lifetime of the installation. This warranty covers defects in workmanship carried out by Amp Me Up Electrical and does not extend to:

  • Faults arising from client-supplied equipment;
  • Third-party tampering or modification;
  • Misuse or normal wear and tear;
  • Storm or flood damage;
  • Events beyond our reasonable control.

This warranty is void if the installation is subsequently worked on, altered, or modified by any person other than Amp Me Up Electrical, including other licensed electricians or trades. Amp Me Up Electrical retains photographic records of completed installations, which will be used to assess any warranty claim; if the installation is found to have been altered or interfered with since completion, the warranty will not apply and call-out, travel, and labour charges will apply to any inspection or rectification. Manufacturer warranties apply to all supplied materials as per the relevant manufacturer's terms.

14.Inconvenience, Power Isolation & Incidental Losses

The client acknowledges that electrical works — and, in particular, emergency fault finding — may require temporary or extended isolation of power, restricted access to certain rooms or areas, and minor disruption to normal household activities. As set out in clause 6, where a fault cannot be safely rectified, power may remain isolated until compliant repairs are completed. Amp Me Up Electrical is not responsible for any inconvenience or incidental loss arising from such disruptions, including but not limited to:

  • Spoilage of food or other perishables during power isolation;
  • Inability to access affected rooms or appliances during works;
  • Missed work or appointments;
  • Loss of heating, cooling, or hot water during works;
  • Any other incidental or consequential loss.

Where extended power isolation is anticipated, the client will be notified in advance wherever practical so that arrangements can be made.

If a split payment plan is agreed, the total amount, payment amounts, and payment frequency will be clearly outlined in the Split Payment Agreement below, and a separate signature is required to confirm acceptance.

18.Exclusions

19.Disposal of Removed Materials

General waste and offcuts generated during the works will be placed in bins provided by the client, including council kerbside waste bins. Where Amp Me Up Electrical is required to remove larger or bulky items — such as cardboard packaging, foam, or other oversized materials — a disposal fee of $30 (excluding GST) will be added to the final invoice. This reflects the flat fee charged to our registered commercial vehicles at our local waste management facility, regardless of the volume disposed of.
